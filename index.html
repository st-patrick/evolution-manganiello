DUMBBELL HAMMER CURL

10-12 reps

  1. Grab a set of dumbbells with a neutral grip so that your palms are facing each other; stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. While bracing your core, curl the dumbbells all the way up to shoulder height while keeping your palms facing each other.
  3. Pause at the top and squeeze your biceps, and then lower the weights back down to the starting position.
